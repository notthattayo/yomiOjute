Skills Development Program

In line with our campaign manifesto, My team and I have put together a FREE skills development Program, with the aim of training members of the ward on technological skills, which are valuable in today's job market and can also equip them to be entrepreneurs. These skills include Web development and Digital Marketing. If you would like to be a part of it, fill the form below to register, and a member of the team would be in touch with you.
